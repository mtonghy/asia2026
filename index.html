Places to go

Dotonbori

★ 4.4Neon · canal

The neon heart of Osaka and the Glico sign — a few minutes' walk from your hotel. Best after dark; eat on the side streets, not the canal front.

Osaka Castle & Park

★ 4.4History

Iconic keep set in a huge moated park. Book tickets online to skip the line; the grounds alone are worth the walk. 9–6.

Umeda Sky Building

★ 4.4Sunset deck

Floating Garden Observatory with a 360° open-air deck — go for sunset into night. Glass-tunnel escalators up are part of the fun.

Tsutenkaku & Shinsekai

★ 4.1Retro · kushikatsu

Old-school Osaka tower with a slide and walk decks, ringed by neon kushikatsu joints. Great for an atmospheric evening.

Tempozan Ferris Wheel

★ 4.5Bay views

Big wheel over Osaka Bay (~¥1000, 15 min), next to the aquarium. Sunset or night for the best ride; clear-floor cabins available.
